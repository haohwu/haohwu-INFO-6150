Culture

Traditional Arts

Experience the elegance of traditional Chinese arts:

  • Beijing Opera: A traditional form of Chinese theater known for its elaborate costumes and stylized performances.
  • Traditional Chinese Painting: Explore the beauty of traditional brush painting, often depicting landscapes, flowers, and birds.
  • Calligraphy: Appreciate the art of expressive writing, considered a form of visual art in China.

Cuisine

Savor the unique flavors of Beijing's cuisine:

  • Peking Duck: Indulge in the iconic dish known for its crispy skin and tender meat, often served with thin pancakes and hoisin sauce.
  • Mongolian Hot Pot: Enjoy a communal dining experience with a simmering pot of broth for cooking various meats and vegetables.
  • Lamb Skewers: Taste the delicious street food featuring grilled skewers of seasoned lamb.

Festivals

Celebrate cultural festivities and traditions:

  • Chinese New Year: Experience the joyous celebration with dragon dances, fireworks, and traditional foods.
  • Mid-Autumn Festival: Join in the festivities of mooncakes, lanterns, and family reunions under the full moon.
  • Dragon Boat Festival: Witness the exciting dragon boat races and enjoy sticky rice dumplings.

Historical Sites

Explore Beijing's rich history at historical landmarks:

  • The Forbidden City: Visit the imperial palace with its grand halls, courtyards, and extensive collection of Chinese art.
  • Temple of Heaven: Experience the architectural beauty of this ancient temple where emperors performed rituals for a good harvest.
  • Summer Palace: Relax in the stunning imperial gardens and lakeside retreat.
